FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about your dental visit or our treatments? Here are answers to the most common queries.

How do I book an appointment? +
Simply call us at +91 70125 90484 or send us a WhatsApp message. You can also fill out the enquiry form on this page and we'll get back to you promptly to schedule your visit.
What are your clinic timings? +
Please call us or WhatsApp to confirm appointment availability, as our timings may vary. Sunday appointments are available on prior booking. We recommend calling ahead to avoid any inconvenience.
Is root canal treatment painful? +
Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is largely painless. Most patients report minimal discomfort during the procedure. Dr. Avin uses advanced techniques to ensure a comfortable experience throughout.
Do you offer braces for adults? +
Absolutely! Orthodontic treatment is available for patients of all ages. Dr. Avin Mathew Kurian, our MDS Orthodontist, will assess your teeth and design a personalised orthodontic plan that suits your needs and lifestyle.
How long does a dental implant take? +
A dental implant typically involves multiple visits over a few months. The implant post is placed first, followed by a healing period, after which the crown is fixed. We'll give you a detailed timeline during your consultation.
Is the clinic hygienic and sterilised? +
Yes. Maintaining a clean and sterile environment is one of our core commitments. All instruments are properly sterilised, single-use items are disposed of after each patient, and we follow strict infection control protocols.
Where is Blooms Dental Care located? +
We are located at No.9, First Floor, Maruthi Nagar, 1st Main Road, opposite Colours Boutique, Madiwala, BTM 1st Stage, Bengaluru, Karnataka 560068. Easily accessible from BTM, HSR Layout, Koramangala, and surrounding areas.
Do you treat children? +
Yes! We welcome patients of all ages, including children. Our gentle, patient-friendly approach makes dental visits comfortable for kids too. Early dental care is important, and we help make it a positive experience.
